# Executive Summary

## App Name: **GameNI** — *Your Court is Waiting*

### One-Liner
A trust-first sports booking and community matchmaking platform for Belfast, built to solve the psychological friction of adult recreational sport.

---

## Vision
GameNI is not a booking app. It is a **trust engine** that converts "playing with strangers" from a high-variance social gamble into a low-variance weekly ritual. We do this by engineering graduated commitment, rewarding organizers, and absorbing the inconsistency of busy adult lives.

## The Problem (In One Paragraph)
Adults in Belfast who want to play sport face a paradox. Supply exists — there are pitches, courts, and pools across the city. Demand exists — thousands of people own gear they never use. But the **transaction cost of trust** kills the market. Showing up alone feels awkward. Committing to a league feels rigid. Organizing a group is thankless. Every existing format — leagues, WhatsApp groups, public park pick-ups — forces a binary choice between **total commitment** and **total chaos**. There is nothing in between.

## The Solution
GameNI fills the diagonal: **Low Barrier + Medium Retention + Medium Social Density**. We do this through three core innovations:

1. **The Lurk Layer:** Users can watch, signal interest, and sub-in without publicly committing. This is how 80% of real-world community formation actually works.
2. **Organizer-as-a-Service:** We treat the person who books the court and chases the money as our most valuable user. We pay them, give them tools, and give them status.
3. **Commitment-Free Reliability:** We guarantee that games happen, even when individuals flake. AI-managed reserves, dynamic waitlists, and no-shame cancellation absorb human inconsistency.

## Target Market
- **Phase 1:** Belfast City (South Belfast / Botanic / East Belfast corridor)
- **Phase 2:** Derry, Lisburn, Antrim
- **Phase 3:** Dublin, UK expansion

## Business Model
- **Freemium:** Free to join and browse. Free to watch and signal interest.
- **Per-Session Fee:** Small booking fee (£1–2) on top of venue cost for confirmed players.
- **Host Subscription:** £15/month for organizers — auto-splits payments, manages waitlists, gives priority booking.
- **Premium Drop-ins:** £5 premium for guaranteed last-minute slots (serves the Inconsistent Intermediate).
- **Venue Partnerships:** Commission on court bookings driven through the platform.
